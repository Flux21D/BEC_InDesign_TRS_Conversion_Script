Guide to Text Complexity

Text complexity dimensions from CCSS are scored on the following scale:

1 Low 2 Middle Low 3 Middle High 4 High

Extended Read 2: “Thurgood Marshall’s Liberty Medal Acceptance Speech”


Informational Text
Quantitative Lexile® 1060L
Qualitative Analysis of Text Complexity
Purpose and Levels of Meaning 3
  • The explicit purpose of this speech is to convince the audience that every American must be brave and work toward justice and equality for all Americans.
Structure 3
  • This persuasive text uses multiple text structures including sequence of events, cause and effect, problem and solution, examples, and descriptions.
Language Conventionality and Clarity 3
  • Contains mostly complex sentences with many higher-level vocabulary terms. Few unfamiliar terms are supported with context clues.
Knowledge Demands 3
  • A general familiarity with U.S. History during the Civil Rights Era, U.S. Government and Citizenship, and the U.S. Constitution is helpful for readers of this text.
